COMMON MISTAKES

RED FLAGS & TRAPS

  • Over-whoring a single stat. Pure strength whores can't hit. Pure dex whores hit for nothing. Pure defense whores get circled by anyone active. No single stat wins fights alone.
  • Staying perfectly balanced forever. With the 2022 soft-cap removal, Special Gyms give meaningful gains. Refusing to unlock them (being stuck 25/25/25/25) leaves free stats on the table.
  • Training without Xanax. 3x Xanax + refill is the biggest single gains multiplier in the game. Stats without Xanax barely keep up with other active players.
  • Ignoring happy. Happy is the biggest non-drug variable in the gym formula. Upgrade your Private Island, use your education bonuses, sell Erotic DVDs for happy, and train when happy is high.
  • Forgetting Dexterity entirely. Dex drives dodge, stealth, and escape. Zero dex means anyone can hit you, reveal you, and keep you pinned.
  • Refilling energy and training cold. Pop Xanax first (or stack happy), THEN refill. Raw refills into low-happy gyms waste gold.
  • Chasing "meta" ratios blindly. Your build should match how you PLAY. A chainer needs strength+speed. A stalemater needs def+dex. Pick a lane.
